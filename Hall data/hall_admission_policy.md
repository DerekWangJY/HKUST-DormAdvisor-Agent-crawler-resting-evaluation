# HKUST Undergraduate Hall Admission Policy (宿舍入住政策)

本文档汇总了香港科技大学本科生宿舍的入住分配政策，包括优先住宿（Priority Housing）、

1.5 Exchange-In students Exchange In students will be provided with University housing for their respective exchange periods, and the University housing application will be arranged by HKUST.

## Part 2a - Hall Point System (积分制度)

### Hall Point System - Criteria and Point Allocation (积分分配标准)

Allocation Policy​ / Undergraduate /

Part 2.a. Hall Point System

Hall places not allocated in the above means will be divided into 2 pools for (i) Local and (ii) Non-Local students/Local students with No-Home-Base in Hong Kong respectively, based on an average ratio of hall offer results of the recent 3 years.

Eligible students, not already provided with University housing by other means, will receive hall points according to the following criteria and be prioritized in their respective pool subject to their Local or Non-Local/ No-Home-Base status, mainly based on the rationale to cater for the housing need of students with Hong Kong home base who live far away from HKUST and to facilitate all students in active campus involvement.

| Section | Allocation Criteria | Max Points (Local) | Max Points (Non-Local/NHB) |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Travelling Time to Campus | 60 | - |
| 2 | Leadership and Contribution to Campus Life | 25 | 50 |
| 3 | Outstanding Achievements | 10 | 30 |
| 4 | No Hall Offer in Previous Year/ Years | 5 | 20 |
| Total | 100 | 100 |  |

## Part 2b - Lottery (抽签)

### Lottery - Non-Local/NHB Random Draw (非本地/NHB随机抽签)

Allocation Policy​ / Undergraduate /

Part 2.b. Lottery

25% of Non-Local/No-Home-Base hall places in the point system will be reserved for random lottery (with equal weighting) in order to provide a certain chance to all third-year or above Non-Local/No-Home-Base eligible hall applicants.

## Part 3 - Waitlist (等候名单)

### Waitlist - Mechanism and Ranking (等候名单机制)

Allocation Policy​ / Undergraduate /

Part 3. Waitlist

Eligible applicants who do not receive a hall offer for the Residential Year at the major round of admission exercise will be placed on 2 separate waitlists according to their respective pools: (i) Local and (ii) Non-Local students/Local students with No-Home-Base in Hong Kong . The waitlist is based on the applicant’s hall point calculated according to the Hall Point System, with a random number assigned to those holding the same points.

Waitlist rankings will be publicized by the Student Housing and Residential Life Office for students’ checking and will be updated regularly. When bed spaces become available in the respective pool, top-up exercises will be conducted, and students on the waitlists will be contacted with a housing offer based on their ranking on the respective waitlists.
